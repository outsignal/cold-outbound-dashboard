# Situ — Company Research

## Company Overview

**Situ** is a global serviced accommodation agency headquartered in **Exeter, England**. Founded in **2008** by Phil Sheridan, the company sources and manages corporate housing across **145+ countries** with a network of **225,000+ units**. They are **privately held** with **~111 employees** and ~12K LinkedIn followers.

Their proprietary platform **Xenia** (built with enterprise tech firm BJSS) handles search, booking, reporting, and integrations for corporate clients.

## Verified Case Studies

1. **London Gaming Company** — Eliminated late-night access crises. Fixed pricing secured. Bookings confirmed within 30 minutes. Scalable process replaced unreliable third-party platforms.
2. **Leading Hedge Fund (London)** — Elite programme: 4.7/5 satisfaction score, 60% SMS feedback response rate, property matching in 25-45 minutes. Personalized touches (pet supplies, groceries, wellness gifts).
3. **Construction Client (UK-wide)** — £24.50/person/night savings vs hotel chains. Edinburgh projects saw 65% total cost savings. Reduced sickness and absenteeism.
4. **Tech Company (Romania → Brisbane)** — Relocated 30 employees + families during Australia's worst rental crisis (0.9% vacancy rate). Partnership expanded to Seattle and other cities.
5. **Arctic Circle & Remote Scotland** — Solved accommodation in locations other providers couldn't service. Only given a map pin reference.
6. **East London Construction** — Cost-effective solutions during peak-season pricing spikes.
7. **Dublin Corporate Relocation** — Navigated one of Europe's tightest rental markets.
