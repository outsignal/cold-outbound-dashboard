# Situ — Ideal Customer Profile

## Target Buyer Titles

- VP/Director of Travel, Travel Manager
- Global Mobility Manager/Director
- Relocation Manager
- Head of Procurement (facilities/travel)
- Office Manager / EA to C-suite (at smaller firms)
- Production Coordinator / Line Producer (entertainment)
- Project Director / Site Manager (construction)

## Target Company Profiles

- TMCs (e.g., CWT, BCD Travel, Corporate Traveller, ATPI, Reed & Mackay)
- Relocation Management Companies (e.g., Cartus, SIRVA, Crown, Santa Fe)
- Enterprise corporates with 500+ employees making regular international assignments
- Construction firms with multi-site UK/EU projects
- Film/TV production companies and studios
- Tech companies scaling internationally (relocating teams)

## Industries

Financial services, tech, construction, film/TV, professional services, energy, consulting

## Geography

UK-first (HQ Exeter), then EU/US/APAC

## Pain Points

1. **Hotels are expensive** — "Your team is overpaying £24.50/person/night on hotel rooms when serviced apartments exist"
2. **Late-night access nightmares** — "Your employees are stranded at midnight because Booking.com hosts don't answer"
3. **No visibility or reporting** — "You're managing corporate housing on spreadsheets and email threads"
4. **Relocation stress** — "Your new hires' first impression of the company is a bad apartment in a rental crisis"
5. **Compliance risk** — "Your current accommodation provider isn't ISO 27001 certified — that's a data risk"
6. **Remote project locations** — "Your teams are sleeping in B&Bs 2 hours from site because nobody else can source rural accommodation"
