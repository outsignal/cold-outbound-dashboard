# Situ — Outbound Messaging Angles

## Angle 1: Cost Savings (Construction / Fit-Out)
"Your team is overpaying £24.50/person/night on hotel rooms when serviced apartments exist. Our construction clients in Edinburgh saw 65% total cost savings — with kitchens, laundry, and proper workspace included."

**Target:** Project Managers, Operations Managers, Procurement at construction/fit-out firms

## Angle 2: Reliability (Corporate / Tech)
"Your employees are stranded at midnight because Booking.com hosts don't answer. We provide 24/7 guest support, fixed pricing, and bookings confirmed in 30 minutes — backed by triple ISO certification."

**Target:** Travel Managers, Office Managers, EAs at corporates

## Angle 3: Relocation Stress (Global Mobility)
"Your new hires' first impression of the company is a bad apartment in a rental crisis. We relocated 30 employees and families to Brisbane during Australia's worst rental crisis — every one of them rated us 4.7/5."

**Target:** Global Mobility Managers, Relocation Managers, HR Directors

## Angle 4: Compliance & Data Security (Enterprise / Financial Services)
"Your current accommodation provider isn't ISO 27001 certified — that's a data risk for your assignees' personal information. We're triple ISO certified with enterprise-grade security built into our Xenia platform."

**Target:** Procurement, Compliance, Travel Managers at financial services and enterprise

## Angle 5: Remote Locations (Infrastructure / Energy)
"Your teams are sleeping in B&Bs 2 hours from site because nobody else can source rural accommodation. We solved housing in the Arctic Circle with just a map pin reference — 145+ countries, 225,000+ units."

**Target:** Project Directors, Site Managers at infrastructure and energy companies

## Angle 6: Channel Partnership (TMCs / RMCs)
"Every extended-stay booking your clients make is an opportunity you're leaving on the table. Partner with us for seamless integrations, competitive rates across 145+ countries, and a dedicated account manager who becomes an extension of your team."

**Target:** Managing Directors, Partnership Managers at TMCs and RMCs

## Tone Guidance
- Consultative, not pushy
- Lead with their pain, not Situ's features
- Use specific metrics (£24.50, 65%, 4.7/5, 30 minutes)
- Mirror Situ's brand: warm but professional
